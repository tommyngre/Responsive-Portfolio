About Me

Woah, you made it to my page. Awesome! I'm Tommy.

I grew up in Chicago and its northern suburbs, and graduated from University of Wisconsin (Madison) in 2007. Since then, I've worked predominately in healthcare IT. For nearly 6, I worked for Epic, an enterprise electronic medical vendor located in Verona, Wisconsin. Currently, I work at LabCorp, a diversified clinical laboratory network, at its headquarters in Burlington, North Carolina, where I certify reverse reference interfaces.
